### Report Topic: Riyadh

---

#### Executive Summary

This report provides an overview of key attractions and cultural sites in Riyadh, Saudi Arabia. The primary focus is on the diverse range of entertainment options available for families and individuals, along with their associated costs. Key findings highlight popular destinations such as Boulevard World and the Riyadh Zoo, which cater to a wide audience including families. Additionally, natural parks like King Abdullah Park offer serene environments suitable for family outings. Cultural institutions, such as the National Museum of Saudi Arabia, provide insight into the country's heritage. Finally, historical landmarks such as Al Masmak Fortress offer free access to history enthusiasts.

#### Research Findings

1. **Boulevard World**
   - **Type**: Entertainment
   - **Target Audience**: Families and individuals seeking fun and excitement.
   - **Cost**: $100 per visit (includes entry fees, food, and activities).
   - **Description**: A themed entertainment park that features roller coasters, water rides, live shows, and interactive exhibits. The park aims to provide a memorable experience with both thrilling attractions and family-friendly zones.

2. **Riyadh Zoo**
   - **Type**: Animals
   - **Target Audience**: Families seeking an educational animal experience.
   - **Cost**: $50 per adult; free for children under 12.
   - **Description**: A zoo that houses a variety of animals, providing visitors with the opportunity to learn about different species and their habitats. Educational programs and interactive exhibits are available to engage both adults and children.

3. **King Abdullah Park**
   - **Type**: Nature
   - **Target Audience**: Families and nature enthusiasts.
   - **Cost**: $20 per entry fee (includes guided tours and parking).
   - **Description**: A beautiful park that offers a tranquil environment for picnics, walking, or simply enjoying the outdoors. The park features lush gardens, walking trails, and recreational areas suitable for families.

4. **National Museum of Saudi Arabia**
   - **Type**: Culture
   - **Target Audience**: Everyone interested in learning about Saudi Arabian history and culture.
   - **Cost**: $20 per adult; free admission on weekends (13:00-20:00).
   - **Description**: A museum that showcases the rich cultural heritage of Saudi Arabia through artifacts, exhibitions, and interactive displays. The museum provides a comprehensive understanding of the country’s historical development.

5. **Al Masmak Fortress**
   - **Type**: History
   - **Target Audience**: History enthusiasts and tourists.
   - **Cost**: Free admission (no entry fees required).
   - **Description**: A historic fort that played a crucial role in Saudi Arabia's unification under King Abdulaziz. Visitors can explore the fortress, learn about its significance, and witness guided tours that provide insights into its historical importance.

#### Recommendations

1. **Enhance Visitor Experience at Boulevard World**
   - **Proposal**: Introduce additional family-friendly activities such as interactive workshops and storytelling sessions.
   - **Reasoning**: This will make the experience more engaging for children and families.

2. **Promote Riyadh Zoo Educational Programs**
   - **Proposal**: Develop and promote educational programs targeting schools and community groups.
   - **Reasoning**: These programs can enhance public awareness of conservation efforts and animal welfare.

3. **Increase Accessibility at King Abdullah Park**
   - **Proposal**: Extend opening hours on weekends and holidays to accommodate more visitors.
   - **Reasoning**: Longer operating hours will increase visitor satisfaction and allow for better utilization of the park’s facilities.

4. **Expand Exhibitions at National Museum of Saudi Arabia**
   - **Proposal**: Regularly update exhibitions with new artifacts and interactive elements.
   - **Reasoning**: This will keep the museum appealing to a broader audience, including young visitors and students.

5. **Strengthen Historical Interpretation at Al Masmak Fortress**
   - **Proposal**: Implement more comprehensive guided tours and historical reenactments.
   - **Reasoning**: These initiatives can make the history of the fortress more engaging and accessible for all visitors.

#### Conclusion

Riyadh offers a diverse array of attractions that cater to various interests, from entertainment parks and zoos to cultural museums and historical landmarks. By enhancing visitor experiences through targeted improvements and community engagement, these sites can continue to attract both domestic and international tourists, contributing significantly to the city’s tourism industry.
